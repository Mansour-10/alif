How do I transfer money to another country?

  • 1 Log in to alif mobi in the "Translations" section.
  • 2 Click "Transfers abroad".
  • 3 Select the desired country from the list.
  • 4 Enter the card number, amount and full name in Latin.
  • 5 The money is already in the account of your loved ones :)
